About Us

HEART OF EVE FOUNDATION

Heart of Eve Foundation is a duly registered NGO based in Lagos, Nigeria and founded on the Christain principle of loving others through acts of service. Our organization aims to show love of Christ in action as we provided needed services and social supports.

Through our education initiative, we have created the Heart School. Through our Heart Schools, Heart of Eve Foundation aims to address the spiritual and educational needs of children and families within our community.

OUR HEART SCHOOL

Our Heart School is a house of worship for children. The purpose of our Heart School is to train the next generation in the things of God while also providing an exceptional education that will prepare students for a life of success.

We aim to develop the whole child and teach academic skills to the highest of standards. We do this by partnering with parents who love Jesus Christ and are looking for an educational partnership in training their children in accordance to the Word of God.

OUR STRATEGY

With the understanding that God's plan for every child is good and that they should be raised according to His laws and statutes; the law of God is the foundation for all pedadogical and theoritical frameworks by which our curriculum is designed.

The coupling of biblical truths and empirically validated teaching techniques and interventions in a truly unique element of our education program.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to demonstrate the excellence and majesty of Christ Jesus by providing a high quality level of service coupled with compassion that will play an essential role in supporting individuals and families in need.

OUR VISION

Heart of Eve Foundation aims to inspire hope and encourage those we serve to work towards goals that will exceed expectations for their lives.
