WHY WE EXIST

Heart of Eve Foundation(Heart of Eve) is a duly registered NGO with the Ngerian Corporate Affairs Commission based in Lagos, Nigeria. It is the Nigerian arm of WAR Ministry Corporation (WAR) based in Newark, New Jersey, USA.

WAR is a non-profit organization founded on the Christian principle of loving others through acts of service. Through Heart of Eve foundation, WAR Ministry Corporation aims to provide Chris-centered services for indiviuals and families in Nigeria.

WHAT WE DO

Our organization aims to show the love of Christ in action as we provide needed services and social supports. Through our education initiative, we have created the Heart School. Through our Heart Schools, Heart of Eve Foundation aims to adress the spiritual and educational needs of children and families within our community.

WHO WE SERVE

Heart of Eve Foundation is founded on the Christian principle of loving others through acts of service. Therefore, we provide a high quality level of service coupled with compassion that plays an essential role in supporting individuals and families in need.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to demonstrate the excellence and majesty of Christ Jesus by providing a high quality level of service coupled with compassion that will play an essential role in supporting individuals and families in need.

OUR VISION

Heart of Eve Foundation aims to inspire hope and encourage those we serve to work towards goals that will exceed expectations for their lives. We want to see the love of Christ in action as we provide Christ-centered services and needed support to families and communities.
