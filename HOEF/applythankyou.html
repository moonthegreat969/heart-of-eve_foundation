Our Services

Educational Services:

COSMAS CHILDREN CENTER

(Our Heart School)

A Heart School is a house of worship for children. The purpose of our schools is to train the next generation in the things of God while also providing an exceptional education that will prepare students for a life of success. We aim to develop the whole child and teach academic skills to the highest of standards. With the understanding that God's plan for every child is good and that they should be raised according to His laws and statutes; the law of God is the foundation for all pedadogical and theoritical frameworks by which our curriculum is designed.

The coupling of biblical truths and empirically validated teaching techniques and interventions in a truly unique element of our education program

The Cosmas Children Center (CCC) is an early childhood center located at 2B Jide Sawyer Drive , Lekki (Ocean Drive), Lagos. The program is designed to help young children develop in the things of God while also receiving a world-class education.

WHO DO WE AIM TO SERVE?

This program is designed for parents who have dedicated their lives to Christ and are looking for an educational partnership in training their children in accordance to the Word of God. The agreement of faith between the home and school is an essential component in meeting the desired outcomes of our program.

There is no set tuition for our creche (7am to 6:30pm) or nursery program (8am to 11pm). Our Teaching Staff

Our Teaching staff are trained to instruct students of all ability levels and each child will get the attention they need to thrive. It is our belief that God has a great plan for every child and our staff is dedicated to seeing that greatness come to pass.

The program incorporates the principles of learning and behavior science, positive psychology and the theory of multiple intelligences in our teaching strategies. All head teachers are dedicated Christains who have commited to studying the Word of God, praying and fasting for your children and family, and continuing their own education. Teachers are selected based on thier experience and commitment to ongoing training in empirically validated effective practices in global education.
